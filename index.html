Safeguarding Community Cats in Singapore

Together with caregivers, residents, and agencies, we build cat-friendly neighborhoods.

About PAWS Watch

We are a grassroots initiative inspired by the AVS Cat Management Framework 2024, championing Trap-Neuter-Return-Manage (TNRM), responsible caregiving, and policy advocacy across HDB estates.

Our Services

TNRM Operations

Humane population control through trapping, neutering, and returning cats under care.

Volunteer Training

We equip caregivers with feeding best practices, sterilisation guidance, and conflict resolution tips.

Education & Outreach

Engaging residents via workshops, school programs, and online content to promote coexistence.
